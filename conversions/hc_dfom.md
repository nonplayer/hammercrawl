# HAMMER Your Way through the Dungeon Full of Monsters

The following pages collect my notes on converting Johnstone Metzger's *Dungeon Full of Monsters* (henceforth referred to as the **DFoM**) to [the HAMMERCRAWL! role-playing game system and gaming process](https://github.com/nonplayer/hammercrawl/). This document is likely to begin life as a collection of unsorted notes, with the hope that it will eventually take a proper format similar to the other docs of the free HAMMERCRAWL! system.


## Why the DFoM?

Because it's awesome, that's why. Metzger's gaming creations have *heavily* inspired the current incarnation of the HAMMERCRAWL! game rules, and the DFoM is the latest entry into that continued font of excellence.

Use good icons to:

* add a treasure roll
* create a pool of advantages for the party
* transform a hostile encounter to friendly


Use bad icons to:

* spawn a random encounter without need for a chance roll
* max the HD amount of any single encounter occupant
* max the "number appearing" of any listed or random encounter
* Create a pool of disadvantages for the party
* spawn a random trap
